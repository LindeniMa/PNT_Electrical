About Us

Interior

Service means keeping homes fire safe which is paramount throughout any building or structure. All assets within the building should be maintained and regularly tested to ensure that everything is SANS 10400 & SABS 1475 compliant.

Exterior

Services and systems supported are geared to undertake installations, repairs, preventative maintenance and provide expert advice to customers to ensure that all their fire control systems conform to the South African National Building Standards at all times.

Mission Statement

High service delivery is our mission, as we would like to maintain a lifelong & valuable relationship with all our valued customers. 'PNT Electrical' does not only strive to meet its commitments & customers' expectations but also aims at exceeding them on every level by delivering good workmanship.

Vision Statement

Our Vision is to perform consistently and deliver value - added services to our customers with the highest level of quality. We also aim at exceeding all our customers' expectations and requirements.

Planning

The Core Value of the company is to demonstrate integrity and transparency in all actions. We belive in conducting business in an ethical manner.

Execution

Our Goal is to keep all our customers in good spirits by rendering an efficient service. PNT ELECTRICAL aims at finding the best ways of optimzing its operations and procedures so that customers benefit and get the best quality of our services, and products in the shortest period of time.
